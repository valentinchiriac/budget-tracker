Delicious Cacao Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cacao powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, cacao powder, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together butter and sugar until creamy.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla extract to the butter mixture and mix well.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients and stir until fully combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips if using.
  7. Shape small dough balls and place them on a baking tray.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until slightly firm but soft inside.
  9. Let cool, then enjoy your homemade cacao cookies!
